# Bad Mergentheim outreach playbook

## Preparation

- D−7: verify hub, routes and lead schema.
- D−6/D−5: refine the five neutral template families.
- D−4: research 20–25 businesses and rank opportunity quality.
- D−3/D−2: verify 10–15 lead briefs, populate demos and add approved media.
- D−1: mobile QA, QR codes, route planning and a 60-second rehearsal.

## Visit week

- Day 1: validate locations and make 2–3 test visits.
- Days 2–4: make 3–4 focused visits daily, outside each business's peak period.
- Day 5: hold follow-up conversations and send permitted proposals.
- Day 6: revisit businesses where the decision-maker was unavailable.
- Day 7: assign every lead a concrete status and next action.

## Door opener

```text
Guten Tag. Sind Sie der Inhaber oder für die Website zuständig?
Ich bin Webentwickler und diese Woche in Bad Mergentheim.
Ich habe unverbindlich ein kurzes Designkonzept für Ihr Unternehmen vorbereitet.
Darf ich es Ihnen in 60 Sekunden zeigen?
```

Show the mobile demo first. Ask how new customers currently find the business and what is missing from its online presence. The first visit aims for a permitted follow-up or scheduled 20-minute conversation, not a detailed negotiation.

## Pricing guidance

Price only after discovery and written scope. If a prospect insists on an immediate range:

- Landing page: €900–€1,300.
- Business website: €1,500–€2,400.
- Growth/integrations: from €2,500.
- Maintenance reference points: €49, €99 or €149 monthly.

A no-upfront-cost model is offered selectively and only with a contractual monthly commitment that covers build, maintenance and delivery risk.

## Before contracting

Confirm German-business invoicing, foreign-currency payment and tax wording with the Serbian accountant. Before public launch, confirm Impressum, privacy, analytics/cookie and processor requirements for the exact production scope.
